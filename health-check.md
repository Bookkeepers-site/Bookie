## Common questions

<div class="hc-faq reveal">
  <details>
    <summary>Why is this free?</summary>
    <p>Two reasons. First, we're building our practice and showing you what we can do is the best marketing we've got. Second, if you like the report and want ongoing help — great, we'll talk. If not, you keep the report. No awkward sales calls.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>What access do you need to my file?</summary>
    <p>Read-only. We can't change anything in your file — we just look. We'll send you step-by-step instructions for inviting us as an advisor (Xero), accountant user (QuickBooks) or read-only user (MYOB). You can revoke access anytime with two clicks.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>Is my data safe?</summary>
    <p>Yes. We're a registered BAS agent, bound by the Tax Practitioners Board code of conduct. We don't share your data with anyone, we don't store copies on our own servers, and we only keep access as long as needed to produce your report.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>How long does it take?</summary>
    <p>From the moment you grant us access, 48 hours. Usually faster. We'll email you the report as a PDF.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>Do I have to sign up for anything afterwards?</summary>
    <p>Nope. You own the report. If you want to keep working with us, we'll offer you 50% off your first month — but only if you ask. No upsell emails, no chase-ups.</p>
  </details>
  <details>
    <summary>What if my books are a mess?</summary>
    <p>Then you especially need this. Part of the report is a clear next-step plan — we'll tell you whether it's a quick tidy-up or a proper catch-up job, and you can decide what to do.</p>
  </details>
